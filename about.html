About Me

Personal Details

I am a motion and graphic designer and developer with a passion for making the creative a reality. I love exploring and developing with new ideas, software, technology and pushing what software, and I, can do. I have a Multimedia Technology and Design Degree at the University of Kent with first class honours and am currently applying that in the broadcast graphics world.

I am passionate about motion and static graphics, 3D, web and the combination of all these different areas. I’m able to design and develop using my knowledge of multiple areas. As technology advances further, graphics are going further from simple renders, and increasingly have a development layer on top utilising data, AI, VR and more. My skillset enables me to sit in this space where creativity and technology meet to make the awesome happen.

In the realm of graphic and motion design, I have been involved in numerous high profile graphic projects for sports organisations and federations, the result being my graphics and designs being seen by millions of people across the world every week on sports broadcasts. These projects have ranged from ground up design projects, to applying existing brands for maximum on screen impact. I would also be responsible for building this in the applicable broadcast system, such as VizRT or Unity. I’ve also used my graphic and motion skills voluntarily for organisations such as Kent Christian Union, Canterbury Vineyard and Hope Community Church.

My skills in web design and development enable me to have a fourth dimension to my work, in that I can use my knowledge of HTML, Javascript, C# and other languages to feed back into my creativity. I’ve helped to develop broadcast graphic software for data feeds and information screens and written a vast amount of script for our Unity graphics system. I've also developed concepts for cloud generated graphics system for social and HTML video overlays, utilising the latest web technologies.
